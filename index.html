About Me

Hello! My name is Evelyn Chao and I am a UX design enthusiast from the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) currently pursuing a career in UX design through the GBDA program. My journey revolves around drawing and creating digital designs using Photoshop and Procreate, which are some of my favourite activities.

In addition to my design work, I enjoy spending time with my dog, Latte, and currently foster cats in Waterloo. Outside of work, I love hanging out in cafes, sipping matcha with friends, and enjoying the calming atmosphere. Swimming and skiing are two of my favourite ways to stay active and embrace the seasons, especially in the crisp fall weather, my favourite time of year!

Fashion is another passion of mine, and I enjoy expressing myself through unique, stylish clothing. I love shopping for new pieces that reflect my personality. Check out my art and projects below to see some of my work!
